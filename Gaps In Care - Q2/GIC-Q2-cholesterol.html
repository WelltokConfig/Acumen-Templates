Focus on a Healthy Heart

[[NameFirst]], keeping your cholesterol in a healthy range helps keep your heart healthy. Do you know your numbers?

If you've had your cholesterol checked within the last year, great! If not, at your next visit, ask your doctor if you are due for a cholesterol check. If you are taking a cholesterol medication, this blood draw will also help your doctor assess how well it is working.

Check your calendar and make sure you have your next appointment scheduled with your doctor and make sure you are up-to-date with your blood work.

Why should you know your cholesterol numbers?
An imbalance between HDL and LDL can increase your risk of a heart attack or stroke, so it's important to know where your levels are. High cholesterol usually doesn't have any symptoms, so checking your levels is extra important!
